FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why should I join Empower Women? By joining Empower Women, you will have access to world expertise, drive innovation with creative ideas and partnerships, enhance your professional visibility, improve your knowledge on women’s economic empowerment and access resources in the Learning Center. Being a member will enable you to: Connect with other members, Share and download resources, Post comments in the Knowledge Library and Knowledge Circles. You will also benefit from learning opportunities in the Learning Center.

How do I get started? To get started and become our community member, please register. Once you have registered, you will receive an email with a verification link.

How do I update my profile? Once you have logged in to Empower Women, you will see your name at the top-hand corner (under the search box). Please click on your name, and then click View Profile. Once you are on your profile page, click Edit Profile to your left.

How do I change my password? Once you have logged in to Empower Women, you will see your name at the top-hand corner (under the search box). Please click on your name, and then click View Profile. Once you are on your profile page, click Change Password to your left.

How can I find the members from my region? You can search the name of your country in the search box at the top. Then click Knowledge Network on the left hand side to filter down to relevant profiles. If there is no member from your country, the Knowledge Network will not be visible.

How can I or my organization engage with Empower Women? There are different ways that you can engage on the Empower Women; a) you can share resources and interesting publications on women’s economic empowerment in the Knowledge Library; b) you join an on-going discussion or start a new conversation on the “Open Ideas for Women’s Economic Empowerment” circle; c) you can ask compelling questions on women’s economic empowerment from your peers and experts in the “Questions to the community” circle; d) you can attend our webinars and access learning materials in the Learning Center. You can invite your colleagues from your organization to join the Empower Women. If you would like to co-host a partner-sponsored discussion and moderate upcoming e-discussions, or would like to know more about partnership modalities, please send us a message at empower.women@unwomen.org

Will Empower Women only be in English? Empower Women is currently available in English only. We will roll-out the platform in other UN languages (Arabic, Chinese, French, Russian and Spanish) soon. However, you can already today upload your resources to the Knowledge Library in your language.

How can I share resources? Locate the Knowledge Library on the landing page and then click on the Explore button. At the top, click Contribute. Detailed instructions are then available.

How can I join a discussion if I am in a different time zone? All discussions in the Knowledge Circles will run without time limits. There are no restrictions and time deadlines for posting comments and creating new discussion entries.

How do I share my ideas on women’s economic empowerment with the Empower Women community? Share your ideas in the “Open Ideas and Questions for Women’s Economic Empowerment” Circle. Do you have an idea or concrete experience about how to empower women economically and improve their economic situations? Make your idea an opportunity visible for everyone and get feedback and suggestions to improve your original idea and start to take action in your community. It is your turn to create this opportunity into a reality for women! Our key values are discussions that are inclusive, collaborative and community-driven.

How can I ask a question to explore in more depth issues around women’s economic empowerment? Share your questions in the “Open Ideas and Questions for Women’s Economic Empowerment” Circle. Are you planning a project proposal on women’s economic empowerment and have questions that you would like to explore in-depth? Or do you have challenges that you would like to share and ask from your peers and our experts? Ask your questions related to women’s economic empowerment here and get support and any referrals to relevant resources and communities. We will mobilize our community to get answers, lessons-learned, success stories and experiences from around the globe.

Who do I contact for technical matters? Please contact us at empower.women@unwomen.org and indicate in email subject line “Technical issue”.
